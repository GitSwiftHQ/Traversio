# Commercial Licensing

Traversio is available under a dual-licensing model:

- community use under `AGPL-3.0-or-later`
- separate commercial licensing for uses that need different terms

## AGPL Use

You may use Traversio under the community license when you comply with the GNU Affero General Public License obligations.

That generally includes:

- preserving the AGPL license terms
- providing corresponding source code when required by the AGPL
- keeping covered derivative work under AGPL-compatible terms when the license requires it
- satisfying the AGPL network-use source-availability requirements

## Commercial Use

Commercial licenses are granted only through a separate written agreement with GitSwift LLC.

Typical reasons to request commercial terms include:

- proprietary or closed-source distribution
- closed-source hosted offerings where AGPL obligations are incompatible with the product plan
- embedding Traversio into a commercial SDK or product under non-AGPL terms
- negotiated warranty, support, indemnity, or other business terms

Until a separate agreement is executed, your rights are governed by the community license in `LICENSE`.
